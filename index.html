AIESEC provides the opportunity for young people to challenge themselves, develop their leadership skills, and make a positive impact on the world - all while being part of a global community that shares their passion for change.

The AIESEC way

AIESEC is aglobal platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through practical experiencesof many kinds, including internships, volunteering opportunities, and more. Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth.

Global Volunteer

Volunteering offers numerous benefits, both for the community and for the volunteers themselves. By volunteering, individuals can make a positive impact on their local community and develop new skills, such as leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving. It's also a great way to explore new interests and hobbies and build one's network of contacts. Finally, volunteering can provide a sense of fulfillment and purpose, and help individuals appreciate the blessings they have.

Global Teacher

Teaching provides numerous benefits, such as the opportunity to make a positive impact on students' lives, access to ongoing professional development, personal and professional growth, and job security. Bahrain's education system is highly respected, and the work of teachers is appreciated. Additionally, teachers in Egypt work in a multicultural environment, have opportunities to learn about Bahraini culture, and can participate in various community events and activities.

AIESEC Member

Being an AIESEC member offers several benefits such as leadership development, international exposure, professional development, networking opportunities, personal growth, and community impact. Members can gain practical work experience, develop new skills, and build relationships with like-minded individuals from diverse backgrounds. They can also contribute to social and economic development and make a positive impact on the world.
